General Treatment Consent
I, the undersigned, consent to receive aesthetic and beauty treatments at SkinLab Medical Spa. I understand that all treatments carry some risk and that results may vary. I confirm that I have disclosed all relevant medical conditions, allergies, and medications to my therapist. I understand that I may withdraw consent at any time before or during treatment. SkinLab Medical Spa reserves the right to decline treatment if it is deemed unsafe or inappropriate.
Personal Data Protection (PDPA)
SkinLab Medical Spa Pte Ltd collects your personal data (name, contact details, medical history, treatment records, photos) for the purpose of providing aesthetic and medical spa services, maintaining treatment records, processing payments, and communicating with you regarding appointments and promotions. Your data will be retained for a minimum of 7 years in accordance with medical record retention requirements. You may request access to, correction of, or withdrawal of consent for your personal data at any time by contacting our Data Protection Officer.
Treatment Photography Consent
SkinLab Medical Spa may take before-and-after photographs for the purpose of tracking your treatment progress and outcomes. These photos are stored securely in your patient record and are accessible only to authorised staff. Photos will not be used for marketing or social media without your explicit separate written consent.
